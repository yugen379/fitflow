Delete your FitFlow account

App: FitFlow  ·  Developer: YUGENTIRAN  ·  Last updated: 2026-06-26

This page explains how to delete your FitFlow account and data. You can delete your account directly in the app, or request deletion by email if you can't access the app.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  • Open FitFlow and sign in.
  • Go to Profile → Settings.
  • Tap "Delete account" (in the danger section).
  • Confirm. Your account and associated data are permanently removed.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't sign in, email fitflow2000@gmail.com from the address on your account with the subject "Delete my FitFlow account". We verify ownership and delete your account within 30 days.

What data is deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, photo, age, height, weight, goals, preferences).
  • Your activity: workouts, meals, water, sleep, mood, weight history, GPS routes.
  • Your community content: posts, comments, and likes.
  • Device identifiers and push tokens linked to your account.

What may be retained, and for how long

We may retain a limited set of records where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or accounting reasons (for example, transaction/billing records). Such data is kept only as long as the law requires and is not used to re-identify you. Backups containing your data are purged within 30 days of deletion.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel a Google Play subscription. Manage or cancel it in the Google Play Store → Subscriptions. See also our Privacy Policy.
